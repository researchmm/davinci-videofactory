Project Description

The DaVinci-VideoFactory project has the potential to automate video production and create video content quickly and easily, without the need for extensive human input. We build a High Resolution (1376√ó768), Watermark Free, and Wide Screen text-to-video generation model, aiming to create new experience for text-driven open-domain video generation.

With our project, you can:

Automatically create video content: With text-driven video generation, it is possible to automatically generate video content from text-based inputs. This can be used to create explainer videos, instructional videos, product demos, and more.

Personalize video content: By incorporating user-generated text input, it is possible to personalize video content to specific individuals or groups. This could be useful in marketing or educational contexts.

Save time and resources: Text-driven video generation can save time and resources in video production by automating the creation of certain types of videos. This can free up human resources to focus on more complex or creative tasks.

Statements

Model Limitations and Biases

Presently, the model is primarily trained on English language corpus and does not offer support for other languages.

The model is trained on public data sets, LAION5B, Webvid-10M, and HD-VILA-100M. The generated results might have deviations related to the distribution of training data.

This model is not capable of producing flawless moive or television quality output. Clear text and position generation is also a limitation of this model.

Risks Associated with Misuse, Malicious Use, and Excessive Use

The model was not designed or trained to create realistic depictions of people or events, and therefore it is not capable of generating such content.

It is strictly prohibited to use the model to create content that is offensive, demeaning, or harmful to people, their environment, culture, religion, or any other aspect of their identity.

The generation of pornographic, violent, or graphic content is prohibited and strictly prohibited.

The model cannot be used to generate content that is erroneous or false, and such usage is also prohibited.
